BLOOD TYPE

A student was asked to find out his parent's blood type. He found out that his mom's blood type is A (represented by Ai on the Punnett Square), and his dad's blood type is O (which is represented by ii on the Punnett Square). However, his bloodtype is AB. Who is really his parent? Are his parents actually his parents? Find out.
